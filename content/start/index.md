Welcome. Before you dig in, let me tell you what this is — and what it isn't.

It's a daily journal written in **easy Levantine (Shami) Arabic**. Short posts about ordinary life, kept plain enough that a learner can actually read them. Not a textbook. Not a course. I'm not a teacher — I'm a guy learning this language as an adult, one نسمة at a time, and writing it down where you can read along.

If you're past the very beginning of Levantine Arabic and you're hungry for **real content that isn't out of reach**, this is for you. And if you're not learning Arabic at all? It's still a daily journal with photos, family notes, and plenty of coffee. You're welcome too.
