I’ll keep adding to this over time, but I’d rather publish a useful shortlist now than wait for “perfect.”

## Quick Advice Before You Book

- Do 1–2 trial lessons, not 10.
- Tell the tutor exactly what you want: “dialect + correction + small homework.”
- If they don’t correct you, move on.
