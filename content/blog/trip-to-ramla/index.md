## خاطِرة

اليوم اضطريت أروح مع ابني يوسف عالرملة. المشوار كان بزهّق، كان عنا جلسة بمكاتب الحكومة. بس الرملة مدينة مختلطة، يعني نص الناس بيحكوا عربي، وبيكيّفوا لمّا يهودي يحكي معهم لغتهم 😀

## كلام

{{< ltr >}}

| Arabic      | IPA                     | Transliteration | Meaning                    | Context / Usage                                                               |
| :---------- | :---------------------- | :-------------- | :------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **اضطريت**  | /idˤ.dˤar.ˈreːt/        | Id-darret       | I had to / I was forced to | Used for obligation. The 'd' is emphatic/deep.                                |
| **مشوار**   | /miʃ.ˈwaːr/             | Mishwar         | Errand / Trip              | Functional trips. "Rehla" is for vacation.                                    |
| **بتزهّق**   | /bit.ˈzah.hiq/          | Bitzahhiq       | Boring                     | From "Zahaq". The 'h' is soft /h/ (like **h**ome).                            |
| **جلسة**    | /'ʒal.se/ or /'dʒal.se/ | Jalseh          | Hearing / Session          | Urban dialect uses /ʒ/ (like **s**u**re**), Jordan uses /dʒ/ (like **j**ump). |
| **مكاتب**   | /ma.ˈkaː.tib/           | Makatib         | Offices                    | Standard plural of maktab.                                                    |
| **الحكومة** | /il.ħu.ˈkuː.me/         | El-Hukumeh      | The Government             | The 'ħ' is deep /ħ/ (sharp 'h').                                              |
| **مختلطة**  | /mux.ˈtˤa.la.tˤa/       | Mukhtalata      | Mixed                      | The 't's are emphatic /tˤ/ (deep 't').                                        |
| **بيكيّفوا** | /bi.ˈkaj.jfu/           | Bikyfu          | They get a kick out of it  | Very local slang. Sounds like "be-kay-yfo".                                   |
| **لغتهم**   | /lu.ˈɣat.hum/           | Loghathom       | Their language             | The 'gh' is /ɣ/ (like a French 'r' or gargling sound).                        |
